About the community

While Ruby on Rails as a framework has one of the strongest tech community around it, the Lagos on Rails community is a collection of enthusiastic developers interested in contributing to the open and international Rails community. The Lagos on Rails community is a growing community, starting from Lagos, with the purpose of creating community support and making strong contributions to the International Rails Community through diverse means. We are able to create community support by learning together at local meet ups where we discuss technical Rails solutions.

We also work closely with nearby Tech companies to have a view of industry demands as they are constantly changing. We are deeply interested in contributing to open source projects and our members are constantly contributing their quota to Open Source, carrying other members along. We learn, we share, we grow and contribute together. We are still growing and will like you to join us for the next meet up.
